Our Mission

The purpose of the CCRA is to connect CT robotics teams with resources such as: Access to full practice field(s) Tools and equipment Support from volunteers & veteran teams Access to industry professionals Educational pathways that lead to job opportunities for students

Our Plan

The CCRA is actively filing the paperwork to become a 501(c)3 non-profit. And will be establishing, supporting, and managing a community robotics center in partnership with a local college. This space will be a collaborative space to work and socialize. We have a specific interest in reaching underserved communities with all our programing.
